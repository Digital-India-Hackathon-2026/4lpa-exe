## Idea
ANTAHDRISHTI is an AI-powered Infrastructure Health Intelligence Platform designed to help governments detect structural defects before they become critical failures. India has millions of public assets such as bridges, flyovers, schools, hospitals, water tanks, and government buildings that rely on periodic manual inspections, allowing cracks, corrosion, and water seepage to go unnoticed. Our platform uses Computer Vision to analyze images captured through smartphones, drones, or CCTV cameras to automatically detect structural defects, assess asset health, generate an Infrastructure Health Score, and prioritize maintenance activities. By enabling predictive maintenance instead of reactive repairs, ANTAHDRISHTI improves public safety, optimizes resource allocation, reduces maintenance costs, extends the lifespan of critical infrastructure, and supports smarter governance aligned with the vision of Digital India.
